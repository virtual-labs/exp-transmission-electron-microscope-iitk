1.Click on simulator tab in  the left side menu<br>
2.Click on "switch on" button to switch on the machine.<br>
3.Select the mode from the dropdown under the heading "choose mode".
4.Drag and drop the available material in machine.<br>
5.Click on vaccum button to set the vaccum.<br>
6.Click on "accelerating voltage" button to set the voltage<br>
7.Hover on various parts of machine to see their names<br>
8.Click on "Go to task page" button to go to task.<br>
